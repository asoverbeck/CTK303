Who is this man?

This fellow right here is Reggie Fils-Aime. He was the president of Nintendo of America since 2006 to 2019. He always seen by many trough memes shared on the internet. He is a very funny individual that has always made me laugh and have a good time, whether it is from him saying "My body is ready!" or "I feel just like a purple pikmin.", he knows what to say at any given moment.

What is he holding?

What Reggie is holding is a Nintendo DS. This handheld device was released on November 21st, 2004 in North America, December 2nd, 2004 in Japan, February 24th, 2005 in Australia, and March 11th, 2005 in Europe. This would be the next handheld-gaming device to be released by Nintendo since the Game Boy.
